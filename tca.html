Site Purpose

The Talent Connect Agency serves as a recruitment resource hub aimed at empowering job seekers in their career pursuits while providing employers with tools and insights to hire effectively.

Scenarios

  • What tools and tips can I use to improve my job application?
  • Where can employers find the best strategies for hiring and retention?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #0056b3 (Headings and links)
  • Secondary Color: #e6f7ff (Backgrounds and accents)

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif; for all headings.
  • Body Font: 'Arial', sans-serif; for all paragraph text.
